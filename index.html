Case Study

Proven Results Across Multiple Industries
GOVERNMENT

AI Powered Excise Goods Registration Platform

We built an AI-powered excise goods registration platform that automates the manual process of extracting product details such as quantities, ingredients, and other attributes from product images and related documents, then performs verification and compliance checks on behalf of tax authorities, ensuring greater efficiency, accuracy, and compliance.

AI Powered Inspection Prediction System

For an entity working closely with the Dubai Municipality, we developed an AI powered solution that predicts which restaurants are most likely to miss grease trap cleanings in the current and upcoming month, enabling inspectors to plan targeted and efficient inspections. This improves compliance, reduces missed violations, and optimizes resource allocation.

Using regression and classification models (Random Forest, XGBoost), time-series forecasting, and geospatial clustering with route optimization (Google OR-Tools), the system scores each outlet based on risk. Instead of relying on trial-and-error scheduling, inspection teams now have data-driven insights to prioritize visits and streamline operations.

AI Powered Automated Calling & Email Engagement System for a Leading Freezone

We built an end-to-end AI-powered engagement and sales automation platform that connects the entire customer journey. The system uses AI voice calls, intelligent email conversations, and real-time agent assistance to automate lead engagement. With RAG, LLM orchestration, and event-based scoring, it generates a Lead Readiness Index to prioritize high-value prospects and tailor follow-up sequences.

The platform identifies buying signals, manages objections, and maintains consistent, data-driven communication across all channels. It also automates proposals, payment links, onboarding, and CRM updates. Built with LiveKit, Deepgram, GPT-4/Claude, ElevenLabs, and event-driven APIs, the solution boosts sales productivity, reduces manual work, and delivers a seamless AI-driven customer experience.

BANKING

Transforming Analytics For A Leading Bank in the UAE

We partnered with a leading UAE bank to streamline and enhance HR decision making by replacing manual Excel-based reporting with real-time Power BI dashboards.

We built a centralized HR data warehouse integrating data from their HRMS, SAP systems, payroll, and compliance tools. This unified data powered interactive dashboards covering Emiratization, recruitment, performance, attendance, and training. With role-based access controls and automated data workflows, the bank's HR team now has accurate, real-time insights and significantly reduced reporting effort.

AI-Powered Document Processing & Workflow Automation

We partnered with a major UAE bank to eliminate manual document handling and accelerate internal processing by deploying an end-to-end AI automation solution.

We built an intelligent document pipeline capable of extracting and classifying information from PDFs, scanned forms, emails, and Excel files used across departments. Using OCR, LLM-based classification, and custom extraction models, the system automatically identifies document types (KYC, bank statements, invoices, HR forms, compliance reports, etc.), validates the data, and pushes it into the bank's core workflow systems.

Automated routing rules, approval triggers, and exception handling replaced slow manual processes. With secure role-based controls, audit trails, and seamless integration with existing banking applications, the bank now processes documents significantly faster with improved accuracy, reduced operational workload, and real-time visibility into all cases.

REAL ESTATE

AI Research and Chatbot Platform for a Leading Developer

We developed a secure, cloud-based AI research assistant that enables employees to query and interact with large organizational knowledge bases in a personalized, role specific manner. The solution uses Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) to deliver accurate responses from uploaded documents and internal knowledge sources, with dedicated dashboards for departments like HR, Finance, and Legal.

Built with React, FastAPI, Azure SQL, Blob Storage, and Cognitive Search, the platform supports multi-tenant architecture, where each business unit maintains its own isolated knowledge base and vector index, while admins retain centralized control. The system includes secure RBAC, section level access restrictions, and ingestion from PDFs, URLs, and SharePoint. Dashboards, reporting, and full UAT were completed before handover.

Duplicate Idea Detection System

We built an AI-powered duplicate idea detection engine for an internal idea submission portal on Joget. Using semantic similarity models (Sentence Transformers) and RAG pipelines, the system identifies meaningfully similar submissions, not just keyword matches, in real time. When a new idea resembles a previously submitted one, users are instantly alerted. This ensures higher-quality submissions, reduces redundancy, and improves innovation management across the organization.

AUTOMOBILE

Discount & Pricing Optimization Model

We built a discount optimization system for one of the largest automotive companies, resulting in a 1.2% increase in volume sales.

The solution combined time-series forecasting (XGBoost), price elasticity modeling taking into account the discount passthrough rate, and a simulation engine to test the possible change in volume on account of changing discount values. By replacing guesswork or trail and error method with data-driven insights, the company can now accurately forecast demand, understand customer price sensitivity, and choose the most effective discount strategies.

INSURANCE

Automated Claims Processing for a Leading Provider

We built an end-to-end AI-powered claims automation system for a major insurance provider to process medical claims.

The platform extracts information from diverse medical documents using OCR and Generative AI, validates it against policy terms, checks eligibility, scores risk, classifies the claim, and routes it to the correct queue, all without human intervention. This significantly reduces manual effort, improves accuracy, and accelerates claim processing by automatically matching diagnoses and procedures with policy guidelines.

AI Powered Document Processing for Manufacturing

We developed an OCR + AI solution to automatically extract technical specifications, such as lengths, dimensions, materials, and part numbers, from PDFs, Excel files, images, and handwritten notes. The system generates structured reports, categorizes data, and forwards it to relevant departments like production etc, eliminating manual entry and enhancing collaboration across engineering, procurement, and production teams.

AUDIT & GRC

AI-Driven Procurement Risk & Anomaly Detection

We partnered with a major UAE retail chain to streamline procurement oversight and reduce financial leakage by implementing an AI-powered anomaly detection and vendor risk scoring platform.

We deployed an intelligent analytics engine that scans purchase transactions, invoices, POs, and GRNs from their ERP in real time. Using machine learning based anomaly detection, the platform automatically flags abnormal pricing, duplicate invoices, quantity mismatches, and unusual charge patterns. For example, if an item normally purchased at AED 12 per unit is suddenly billed at AED 28, or if a PO and invoice show mismatched quantities, the system instantly highlights the discrepancy for review, preventing over billing and process gaps.

We also built a dynamic vendor risk scoring model that continuously evaluates suppliers based on delivery timelines, product quality issues, historical audit findings, return rates, and payment behavior. The risk score updates automatically as new data flows in. For instance, a supplier with frequent late deliveries and repeated credit note adjustments may see their score rise from 40 to 82, while consistent improvement in on time performance lowers risk back into a safe range.

With automated alerts, integrated dashboards, and unified data visibility across procurement, finance, and operations teams, the retail chain now proactively identifies risks, reduces operational leakages, and improves supplier performance, all powered by real-time AI insights.
